Our Story

Khmer Gallery started as a simple idea: a place to find good animal photos without digging through endless search results. Every photo is sorted into a category, so you can go straight to birds, cats, dogs, or cattle.

The site is free to browse and free to download from. No account, no sign-up, no hidden steps.

Good to know

Free to use — every photo can be downloaded for personal use.

Simple to browse — photos are grouped by category, not by upload date.

Always growing — new categories are added over time.
